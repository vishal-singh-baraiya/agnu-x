Fueling Innovation

We are a student-led society passionate about fueling innovation and discovery.

‍

By funding startups and research, we provide aspiring entrepreneurs and researchers the opportunity to turn their bold ideas into reality. Together, we aim to create a culture where creativity thrives, innovation flourishes, and boundaries are pushed. Through collaboration, mentorship, and support, we’re not just funding ideas—we’re building a future shaped by the vision and determination of our peers. Let’s create, innovate, and inspire, together.

Advocating for

Bold Ideas

Our mission is to champion the dreams and ambitions of students and researchers. We believe that innovative startups and groundbreaking research deserve the resources and support to thrive. Through collaborations with mentors, academic institutions, and industry leaders, we are creating opportunities to turn ideas into reality and advocating for a future where creativity and innovation flourish.
